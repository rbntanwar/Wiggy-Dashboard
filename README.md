# 🍕 WIGGY Food Delivery — Power BI Dashboard

- Built a **two-page interactive Power BI dashboard** to monitor and analyse the performance of a food delivery platform covering sales, delivery, and customer behaviour
- Designed a **star-schema data model** with four related tables — Sales Data (fact), Food Details, Customer Details, and Restaurant Details
- Created **3 core DAX measures** — Total Quantity, Transactions, and Average Quantity — powering all KPI cards across both pages
- Used **Power Query** for data transformation and cleaning before loading into the model
- **Page 1 (Top Analysis)** — KPI cards, Top 10 foods bar chart, monthly column & line charts, delivery status donut chart, payment method funnel, and food type vs. member type pivot matrix
- **Page 2 (Bottom Analysis)** — Bottom 5 foods bar chart, stacked area chart, delivery status treemap, and an expanded pivot matrix with Transactions + Total Quantity
- Implemented **6 cross-filtering slicers** — Month, Member Type, Payment Method, Food Type, Restaurant Type, and Month & Year — updating all visuals simultaneously
- Added **navigation action buttons** for seamless page switching between both dashboard pages
- **Data Source** — Skill Course Power BI series by **Satish Dhawale**
